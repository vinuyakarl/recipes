Pizza Recipe

Pizza is a popular and beloved dish that originated in Italy but has gained worldwide recognition. It consists of a round, flat dough base topped with various ingredients and baked in an oven. The dough is typically made from flour, water, yeast, and salt, and it undergoes a process of fermentation to develop flavor and achieve a light and crispy texture. The toppings can vary widely, ranging from classic options like tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil, to a wide array of meats, vegetables, and other flavorful additions. Once assembled, the pizza is baked until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbling. Pizza is enjoyed for its delicious combination of flavors, textures, and versatility, making it a favorite choice for casual meals, gatherings, and even special occasions.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature possible (typically around 500F or 260C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat as well.
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the pizza dough into your desired thickness. Transfer the rolled dough to a baking sheet or a pizza peel lined with parchment paper.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  4. Arrange the sliced mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Tear a few fresh basil leaves into small pieces and scatter them over the cheese.
  6. Drizzle a little extra-virgin olive oil over the pizza. Season with Salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Carefully transfer the pizza (on the baking sheet or pizza peel) to the preheated oven or onto the hot pizza stone, if using.
  8. Bake the pizza for about 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove the pizza from th eovern and let it cool for a few minutes. Cut into slices and serve hot.
